01 
What Is Nivedan AI?
Nivedan AI is an autonomous, multi-agent SaaS platform that transforms the way agencies, freelancers, and sales teams respond to RFPs - Request for Proposals. The name comes from the Hindi and Sanskrit word निवेदन, meaning a formal, respectful submission - which is exactly what an RFP response is. A professionally written proposal submitted to win a contract, a government tender, or a large enterprise deal. 
Built on Google Agent Development Kit (ADK) with TypeScript, Nivedan AI deploys six highly specialised AI agents that work in a coordinated pipeline - reading the uploaded RFP document, researching the client company, matching your capabilities to their requirements, writing a full tailored proposal, reviewing it for completeness, and exporting a professionally formatted PDF - all in under 20 minutes. 
The platform runs entirely within a single Next.js 15 monorepo. All agents execute inside Next.js API routes powered by Gemini 3.1 models - no separate backend server, no external infrastructure. Authentication is handled by Clerk, data by Neon PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM, and final reports are delivered by email via Resend. The result is a production-grade enterprise tool deployed to Vercel as a single application. 
Nivedan AI does not generate generic proposals. Every proposal it creates is deeply tailored to the specific client - using real-time research about that company, their recent news, their industry priorities, and the exact requirements stated in their RFP. It is the difference between a 20% win rate and a 50% win rate.

03 
The Six Agents - What Each One Does
Nivedan AI orchestrates six specialised agents in a coordinated pipeline. Each agent has one clearly defined job. The Orchestrator manages session state and routes tasks. The remaining five agents execute in sequence, each one passing its structured output to the next - building the final proposal layer by layer. 
01
Orchestrator Agent
THE BRAIN · ROOT ADK AGENT
gemini-3.1-pro
The Orchestrator is the root agent that manages the entire pipeline. When a user uploads an RFP and submits their job, this agent creates the session state, reads the uploaded file reference and the company profile, and decides the exact execution sequence of all downstream agents. It handles errors gracefully - if an agent fails, it retries or skips and continues the pipeline rather than crashing the entire workflow. It is the conductor that ensures all six instruments play in the right order. 
REAL EXAMPLE
User uploads a 38-page hospital RFP and clicks "Generate Proposal." The Orchestrator reads the file metadata, creates a session with the job ID and company profile, and begins triggering agents in sequence - starting immediately with the RFP Parser Agent.
OUTPUT Structured session state object shared across all agents 
02
RFP Parser Agent
DOCUMENT INTELLIGENCE · REQUIREMENT EXTRACTOR
gemini-3.1-flash-lite 
This agent reads the entire RFP document - no matter how long, no matter how poorly structured - and extracts every critical piece of information from it. It identifies mandatory requirements versus optional ones, pulls out the budget ceiling, project timeline, evaluation criteria, preferred vendor qualifications, and any compliance or certification requirements mentioned. It converts a chaotic 40-page document into a clean, structured JSON object that every other agent can reliably reference. 
REAL EXAMPLE
The RFP says on page 12 - "Vendor must have HIPAA compliance experience." On page 28 - "Budget not to exceed $150,000." On page 5 - "Delivery within 6 months." This agent finds all three, regardless of where they appear in the document, and marks them as mandatory requirements with priority flags.
OUTPUT Structured RFP Blueprint JSON - mandatory requirements, budget, timeline, evaluation weights 
03
Client Research Agent
COMPANY INTELLIGENCE · CONTEXT BUILDER
gemini-3.1-flash-lite
This agent researches the company that sent the RFP using the company name extracted from the document. It searches the web for recent news, funding rounds, leadership changes, product launches, expansion plans, and stated strategic priorities. This intelligence is what makes the resulting proposal feel like it was written by someone who deeply understands the client - not a generic template. A proposal that references a client's recent initiative is always more compelling than one that does not. 
REAL EXAMPLE
The RFP is from Apollo Hospitals. This agent discovers that Apollo recently announced a ₹2,000 crore investment in digital health infrastructure and is expanding their telemedicine platform to Tier 2 cities. The proposal now opens with: "Given Apollo's strategic focus on digital health expansion into Tier 2 markets, our solution directly addresses your infrastructure modernisation goals..."
OUTPUT Client Profile JSON - company context, priorities, recent news, industry position 
04
Requirements Matcher Agent
CAPABILITY ALIGNMENT · PROOF FINDER
Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite 
This agent compares what the client needs against what your company actually offers - and finds the strongest evidence for each match. It searches your company knowledge base - past proposals, case studies, team bios, certifications, technology stack documentation - and maps every RFP requirement to your most relevant proof point. If you have delivered something similar before, this agent finds it and connects it to the right section of the proposal. No requirement goes unaddressed. 
REAL EXAMPLE
Client requires HIPAA compliance experience. The knowledge base contains a 2024 case study where your team built a HIPAA-compliant patient portal for a US clinic. This agent retrieves it, extracts the key metrics - 10,000 patients migrated, zero compliance violations, delivered 3 weeks early - and maps it directly to the requirement. The proposal now has real evidence, not empty claims.
OUTPUT Capability Match Table - requirement to proof point mapping with confidence scores 
05
Proposal Writer Agent
FULL DOCUMENT DRAFTER · SECTION BUILDER
gemini-3.1-pro
This is the core creative agent. It takes all structured outputs from the previous three agents - the parsed requirements, the client research, and the capability matches - and writes the complete proposal document from scratch. It generates every standard section: Executive Summary, Understanding of Requirements, Proposed Solution, Technical Approach, Case Studies, Team & Expertise, Project Timeline, and Pricing Structure. Every sentence is written with the specific client in mind. Gemini 3.1 Pro is used here because this task demands the highest level of language reasoning, context retention, and coherent long-form writing. 
REAL EXAMPLE
The Executive Summary opens with Apollo's digital health mission, acknowledges their Tier 2 expansion initiative, and immediately connects it to your team's healthcare technology track record - all in the first three sentences. The Proposed Solution section maps each of their eight stated requirements to a specific feature of your system with supporting evidence from past projects. No generic filler.
OUTPUT Full proposal draft - all sections written, structured, and ready for review 
06
Quality Review & Export Agent
EDITOR · VALIDATOR · PDF GENERATOR
gemini-3.1-flash-lite
This agent acts as a senior editor and quality gate. It reads the complete draft proposal against the original RFP requirements and checks that every mandatory requirement has been addressed, that the budget is respected, that the timeline aligns with the client's deadline, and that no section contains conflicting information. It flags issues, corrects them, and then hands the final approved document to the export module which formats it into a professionally branded PDF - with your company's logo, colors, table of contents, cover page, and page numbers - ready for immediate submission. 
REAL EXAMPLE
The draft says the project will be delivered in 8 months. The RFP required 6 months. This agent catches the conflict, revises the timeline section, and updates the project milestones to fit within 6 months. Then exports a clean, professional PDF with your agency's branding applied automatically from your profile settings.
OUTPUT Submission-ready branded PDF proposal + dashboard view + Resend email delivery
